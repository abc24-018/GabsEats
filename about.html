About PutYouOn

Connecting Gaborone residents with hidden food gems since 2023.

Our Mission

At PutYouOn, we're passionate about food and our local community. Our mission is to connect food-loving residents of Gaborone with the incredible hidden food spots that make our city special.

We believe that great food doesn't always come with a fancy facade or expensive marketing. Sometimes, the best culinary experiences are found in those small, unassuming places that locals cherish but visitors might miss.

By creating a platform that highlights these hidden gems, we aim to:

  • Support local small businesses and entrepreneurs
  • Promote food diversity and cultural exchange in Gaborone
  • Create a community of food enthusiasts who share their discoveries
  • Make it easier for everyone to find great food, regardless of budget
